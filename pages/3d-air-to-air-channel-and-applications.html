3D Air-to-Air Channel and Applications

< back to Projects

Overview:

Our group at SMU conducted several in-field measurement campaigns to study the effect of the drone body and 3D positioning on the Air-to-Air (A2A) Channel between multiple drones. These measurements have been used to model potential applications of multi-UAV systems such as distributed beamforming.

Publications:

  • 2022 IEEE Latincom Effect of Antenna Orientation and UAV Position on UAV Communications in 3D Space [pdf] (Best Paper Award Winner!)
  • 2022 IEEE CCNC Leveraging UAV Rotation To Increase Phase Coherency in Distributed Transmit Beamforming [pdf].
  • 2021 IEEE WoWMoM Swarmnet Workshop Effect of Antenna Orientation on the Air-to-Air Channel in Arbitrary 3D Space [pdf].
